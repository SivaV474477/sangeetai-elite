However, I can't directly embed your personal photos and videos into the project files from here. Here's how to do it yourself in just a few steps:
Step 1 — Copy your media files into the project
bash# Create a media folder in public/
mkdir -p public/media

# Copy your files there (adjust paths to where your files are)
cp "WhatsApp_Image_2026-04-20_at_7_42_21_PM.jpeg" public/media/studio-hero.jpg

Step 2 — Open src/pages/RagaLibrary.jsx and add this hero section at the top of the return statement:
jsx<div className={styles.heroMedia}>
  <video autoPlay muted loop playsInline className={styles.heroBg}>
    <source src="/media/studio-video.mp4" type="video/mp4" />
  </video>
  <img src="/media/studio-hero.jpg" alt="Studio" className={styles.heroPhoto} />
</div>
Step 3 — Add these styles to RagaLibrary.module.css:
css.heroMedia {

  border: 1px solid var(--gold-border);
}
.heroBg {
  width: 100%;
  height: 100%;

  filter: sepia(0.3);
}
.heroPhoto {
  position: absolute;
  bottom: 20px;
